Virtualization & System Level Design Group

Objectives

At our Virtualization & System Level Design Group, we are working on researches and development about cooperative design environment, and dynamic reconstruction, also as well as SafeG, which is the virtualization environment for embedded systems. By proposing a solution of flexible system design technique for both software and hardware, we provide a SafeG-based virtualization infrastructrue.

Research Topics

  • Dynamic Reconstruction
  • Cooperative Design Environment
  • Virtualized Environment for Embedded Systems
