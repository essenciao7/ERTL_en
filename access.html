Access to TAKADA Labratory

TAKADA Labratory is located at east side of IB Building, south wing, Higashiyama campus, Nagoya University.

We recommend you to take the subway's Meijo Line directly to Nagoya University Station. If you are coming from Nagoya Station, you may take the Higashiyama line first, and transfer to Meijo Line at Motoyama Station.

To get to the south wing of IB Building, get out of the subway station from exit no.3, you will see a 5-floor building at your right hand, although the 1st floor is a semi-underground position, you may only see 4 floors of the building. The entrance is on the south side of the building. You may get out from exit no.3, get along the road, and turn right at the first corner, and enter the compus.
